Aquarium Automation System

I built this system so I didn't have to manually turn on and off the lights for my turtle, and if I wanted to, I could control the lights from anywhere in my house. I also wanted to simulate natural sunrise and sunset cycles by controlling the light based on date and season.

Hardware

I used an ESP32-C6 board from Seeed Studio XIAO series, for its compact size and native support for Wifi 6 and external antennas. For controlling the lights, I used a standard 5V low level trigger relay with optical isolation rated for 300W. I connected the relay to a power strip (I didn't want to cut up my lights) that powered on a 75W heat lamp and a 10.0 15W UVB bulb.
