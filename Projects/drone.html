Long range fpv drone

The goal of this project was to build a long range fpv drone for filming videos and such. I wanted to make the drone as un-losable as possible. I wanted GPS return to home and position hold, so if I lost signal the drone could try to fly back by itself. I also wanted features like GPS telemetry logs and beepers, so if the drone crashed, I would have the approximate coordinates and the beeper would make noise and help me find it.

Parts

Frame: Phisital Aries4 4 inch foldable frame

I chose to go with a 4-inch sized frame because they have a good balance of efficiency and weight. While 5-inch drones are usually pretty heavy (like 750g+), 4-inch drones are usually just stretched out 3-inch drones, and it's reasonable for them to weigh around 250g, and the extra propeller diameter is more efficient.
