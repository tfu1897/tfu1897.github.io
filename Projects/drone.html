Long range fpv drone

The goal of this project was to build a long range fpv drone for filming videos and such. I chose to go with a 4-inch sized frame because they have a good balance of efficiency and weight. While 5-inch drones are usually pretty heavy (like 750g+), 4-inch drones are usually just stretched out 3-inch drones, and it's reasonable for them to weigh around 250g, and the extra propeller diameter is more efficient.
